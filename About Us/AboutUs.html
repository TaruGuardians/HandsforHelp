About Us

It is easy to feel powerless in the face of covid-19 but here we are to make the difference.HandsforHelp is a small initiative sponsored by TaruGuardians to help people get information regarding beds, oxygen, food, plasma, etc. We only compile information that the government provides or is already available in the public domain, including the internet (social media). We are neither Covid healthcare service providers nor do we hoard any medicines, oxygen cylinders, or any other supplies. We cannot confirm the price of goods and services mentioned on our website. Due diligence and exercise of cautious judgment are strongly advised. Please use the information wisely and do not associate it with illegal, unethical, immoral, and unlawful activities. Please verify the information independently
